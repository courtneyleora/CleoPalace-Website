About midwife care

Midwife care is provided by trained healthcare professionals known as midwives. Midwives are specialized in supporting women through pregnancy, childbirth, and postpartum recovery. They are trained to handle normal, low-risk pregnancies and births, and they focus on a holistic approach to maternal care, emphasizing natural childbirth when possible.

After childbirth, midwives continue to care for the mother and newborn, providing support for breastfeeding, postpartum recovery, and infant care.
